Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 4th September 2023

Great Ape is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

Website Analytics

When you visit our website, we may collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit. We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and better understand user behavior. Google Analytics collects information about your location, device, and browsing activity. This data is aggregated and anonymized, and it helps us improve our website's content and functionality.

Joining Great Ape

If you choose to join Great Ape by completing our registration form, we will collect personal information, including your name, email address, and responses to questions about your interests and objectives. This information is used to create a user profile and provide you with access to our community-driven projects and resources.

How We Use Your Information

  • To improve our website's content and user experience.
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior.
  • To communicate with you about updates, events, and opportunities related to Great Ape.
  • To provide you with access to our community-driven projects and resources.
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests.

Hosting and Security

Our website is hosted on Microsoft Azure, a trusted and secure cloud computing platform. Microsoft Azure provides a high level of security to protect your data. Our website is hosted as a static web app within a storage account, which offers secure and reliable hosting for our content.

While we take measures to ensure the security of your information, it's important to remember that no online platform can guarantee absolute security. We continuously monitor and update our security practices to mitigate risks and protect your data.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may share your information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website or providing services to you, but they are obligated to keep this information confidential.

Your Consent

By using our website and joining Great Ape, you consent to our Privacy Policy and the collection and use of your information as described herein.

Your Choices

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On. You can also manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact us at @GreatApeOrg
